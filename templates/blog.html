About Safi Water Billing System

The Safi Water Billing System is designed to provide a seamless experience for customers to manage their water usage, track billing, and make payments efficiently.

Features

  • Billing Management: View and track monthly water bills.
  • Payment Processing: Secure online payments.
  • Water Consumption Tracking: Monitor daily and monthly water usage.
  • Feedback & Support: Submit complaints and queries.
  • Report Issues: Easily report water-related problems.

User Management

The system ensures that customers, administrators, and support staff have access to the functionalities that suit their roles:

  • Customers: Can log in, check bills, pay online, track usage, and report issues.
  • Administrators: Manage customer accounts, update billing records, and analyze usage trends.
  • Support Team: Handle customer complaints and feedback effectively.

Security Measures

The system follows strict security guidelines to ensure data protection:

  • Encrypted database and secure payment gateway.
  • Role-based access control for different users.
  • Regular backups to prevent data loss.
  • Secure login with password hashing.
